About Us

 

The institute has been established in the year 2004 with a view to serve the knowledge of foreign language. Our aim is to address the needs of educational and business communities and to promote the language diversity. This is the only institute in India which is non-European member of European Union Institutions. Institute has a very good faculty who are well experienced in teaching foreign languages. We have very good language resources. Our services include Training, Translation and Immigration Consultancy

 

The Founder of the Institute is Beloved (late) Rev. Sr. Fernande Marie (Switzerland) and The Director of the Institute, Michael Anil Betha has great experience with French Language and worked with various Institutions like St. Joseph’s Organization, Visakhapatnam and EMAP of France. For past 25 years he has been working with French People in social service activities and holds P.G Degree in French Literature from BU.
